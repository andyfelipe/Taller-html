duvic

Duvic is a Spanish brand of children's footwear that was born in Elche (Alicante) in 1972. In the more than 30 years it has been in the sector it has come to export its products throughout Europe. In their manufacture they use leather and natural cotton materials, they are sustainable and ecological looking for the environment, since this is a subject that more and more customers appreciate. The Duvic shoes have the rubberized volcanized sole, this makes them more resistant to wear and provide longer duration. Duvic opts for the design and also for the comfort, making it the perfect combination.
